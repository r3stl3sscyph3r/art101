An everyday task: Baking cookies

  1. Find a recipe
  2. Gather your ingredients, such as flour, butter, eggs and any extras
  3. Following the recipe, assemble the dough
  4. Pre-heat your oven
  5. Bake for the specified time
  6. Remove cookies and allow to cool
  7. enjoy
